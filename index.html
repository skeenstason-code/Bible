The One Story That Leads to Jesus

A daily Bible reading plan you can begin at any time.

Day

Short Prayer Office

The Opening

Lord, open our lips.

And our mouth shall proclaim your praise.

Glory to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit:

as it was in the beginning, is now, and will be forever. Amen.

Except in Lent, add: Alleluia!

Confession of Sin

Let us confess our sins against God and our neighbors.

Almighty God, our heavenly Father: We have sinned against you, through our own fault, in thought, and word, and deed, and in what we have left undone. For the sake of your Son our Lord Jesus Christ, forgive us all our offenses; and grant that we may serve you in newness of life, to the glory of your Name. Amen.

May the Almighty God grant us forgiveness of our sins, and the grace and comfort of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

Psalm

The Psalm appointed for the day now may be read.

After the Psalm is read; say:

Glory to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit:

as it was in the beginning, is now, and will be forever. Amen.

Scripture

The appointed Reading from Scripture may now be read.

After the reading, say:

The Word of the Lord.

Thanks be to God.

The Lord's Prayer

Our Father, who art in heaven, hallowed be thy Name, thy kingdom come, thy will be done, on earth as it is in heaven. Give us this day our daily bread. And forgive us our trespasses, as we forgive those who trespass against us. And lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil. For thine is the kingdom, and the power, and the glory, for ever and ever. Amen.

All are invited to offer their own prayers and thanksgivings here.

Thanksgiving

Most merciful Father, we thank you for your goodness and loving-kindness to us and to all you have made; thank you for our Redeemer, Jesus Christ, and our Advocate, the Holy Spirit. Grant us thankful hearts, that we may praise you not only with our lips but in our lives, and walk in your way of love all our days; through Jesus Christ our Lord, who with You and the Holy Spirit lives and reigns in glory for ever and ever. Amen.
